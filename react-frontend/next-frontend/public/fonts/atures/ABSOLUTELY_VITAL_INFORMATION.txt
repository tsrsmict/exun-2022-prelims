Atures is a high quality multi-weight typeface, drawn and created by Måns Grebäck during 2018.
It is a professional font family with futuristic, space-inspired design. 

The family consists of 12 weights and with italic a total of 24 styles.

Atures has extensive language support, and contains all characters you'll ever need.
